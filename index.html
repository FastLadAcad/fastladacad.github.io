Questions

Frequently asked

What is Fast Lad Academy?

Data and video coaching for track drivers: a full curriculum, corner-by-corner session reviews from coaches who've raced at a national level, live Driver's Meetings and Live In-Car sessions, and a real community that doesn't pack up when the track day ends.

How is this different from a weekend racing school?

A weekend is one shot, a long wait, and a big bill. We're ongoing: learn the craft, submit real sessions from your own car, and get coached again and again.

We don't compete with track time — we make every lap you've already paid for worth more.

I'm a beginner / I'm already quick — is this for me?

Both. Beginners get a structured path from the ground up. Experienced drivers can submit a session any time — you never have to sit through coursework you already know to get coached.

What do I need to submit a session?

In-car video from your last track day or sim run. A data/telemetry export from any logger makes the review sharper, but it's optional — your video alone is plenty to get started.

How fast do I get my review back?

Typically within 48 hours — often before your next event, while the laps are still fresh.

What's the Founding 500?

The first 500 members lock in 25% off their tier — for life, as long as they stay subscribed. Only 500 founding memberships will ever exist. After that, standard pricing applies.

What cars do you coach?

Launch coverage spans Mustang, Civic, Camaro, Corvette, Miata, Porsche, S2000 and more — with coaches who've driven your platform at speed. Don't see yours? Join anyway: your platform goes on the coverage map, and that's how we recruit the next coach.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — it's a monthly membership, not a contract. One note: the founding rate holds only while your membership stays active, so cancelling forfeits the 25%-off-for-life lock.
